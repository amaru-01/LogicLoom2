About LogicLoom

We are the world's largest tech and shop startup

LogicLoom is a leading technology retailer in Kenya, renowned for offering an extensive range of high-quality phones, laptops, and tech accessories. As a trusted name in the tech industry, we are committed to bringing the latest innovations from top brands to our customers, ensuring they have access to cutting-edge technology. Our product portfolio includes the newest smartphones, high-performance laptops, and a diverse array of accessories such as chargers, cases, and headphones, all meticulously selected to meet the varying needs of tech enthusiasts, professionals, and everyday users alike.


At LogicLoom, we believe that exceptional customer service is as important as the products we offer. Our dedicated team of knowledgeable and friendly staff is always ready to provide expert advice and personalized recommendations, helping customers make informed decisions. We understand that purchasing technology can be a significant investment, which is why we strive to make the shopping experience as seamless and enjoyable as possible. From the moment you walk into our store or visit our website, you can expect attentive service, detailed product information, and a commitment to ensuring your complete satisfaction.


In addition to our extensive product offerings, LogicLoom provides a range of support services designed to enhance your technology experience. Our team offers setup assistance, software installation, and troubleshooting to ensure your new devices are ready for immediate use. We also provide comprehensive repair services, with skilled technicians capable of handling everything from screen replacements to hardware upgrades. At LogicLoom, our mission is to be more than just a retailer; we aim to be your trusted technology partner, dedicated to keeping you connected, productive, and ahead of the curve with the latest tech advancements.
