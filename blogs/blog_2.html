What is inside our Tech shop

LogicLoom is a premier tech shop in Kenya, specializing in the sale of top-quality phones, laptops, and a wide range of tech accessories for both. We pride ourselves on offering the latest models from leading brands, ensuring our customers have access to the best technology on the market. Whether you're looking for the newest smartphone, a high-performance laptop, or essential accessories like chargers, cases, and headphones, LogicLoom has you covered. Our extensive selection is curated to meet the diverse needs of our customers, from tech enthusiasts to professionals seeking reliable devices for work.


In addition to our comprehensive product range, LogicLoom is committed to providing exceptional customer service. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are always on hand to help you find the perfect device or accessory, offering expert advice and personalized recommendations. We understand that purchasing tech products can be a significant investment, and we strive to make the process as smooth and informative as possible. Our goal is to ensure that every customer leaves our store satisfied, confident in their purchase, and equipped with the technology they need to succeed in their personal and professional lives.


Beyond sales, LogicLoom offers a range of support services to enhance your tech experience. We provide setup assistance, software installation, and troubleshooting to ensure your new devices are ready to use right out of the box. Our repair services are available for those times when your tech needs a little extra care, with skilled technicians who can handle everything from screen replacements to hardware upgrades. At LogicLoom, we are dedicated to being more than just a tech shop; we are your trusted partner in technology, committed to helping you stay connected and productive with the latest and greatest in tech innovations.
